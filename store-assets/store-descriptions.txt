===== APP NAME =====
RetroRush

===== SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max) =====
10 retro micro-levels of pure 90s arcade action. Can you beat them all?

===== FULL DESCRIPTION (Google Play) =====
RetroRush is a love letter to the golden age of arcade games — 10 unique micro-levels packed with retro-style gameplay, each one a different challenge inspired by the classics of the 90s.

🎮 LEVELS INCLUDE:
• Climb towering floors on moving platforms — don't fall!
• Pop bouncing orbs with your harpoon before time runs out
• Pilot your ship through a deadly tunnel, Flappy Bird-style
• Smash bricks with a paddle in classic breakout action
• Pull falling items with your claw to reach $500
• Survive a zombie arena with your joystick and blaster
• Navigate a scrolling maze and reach the EXIT alive
• Outrun a collapsing wall in a heart-pounding sprint
• Dodge bullets in space and survive 20 seconds
• Aim and launch projectiles to destroy every target

⚡ FEATURES:
• Mobile-first touch controls — joysticks, drag pads, and tap-to-play
• 10 hand-crafted levels, each with a unique mechanic
• Fast restarts — no waiting around
• Retro pixel art style
• Totally free, no ads, no in-app purchases

🏆 Think you can clear all 10 levels?
RetroRush is quick to pick up, tough to master. Perfect for short gaming sessions or marathon runs.

More levels coming soon — we're building all the way to 50!
